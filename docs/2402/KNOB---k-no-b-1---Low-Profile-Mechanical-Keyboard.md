# KNOB / k.no.b.1 / Low Profile Mechanical Keyboard

> 来源：[https://knob.design/](https://knob.design/)

The Knob / k•no•b•1 is a low-profile mechanical keyboard being designed by 3D Artist and Motion Designer [Ben Fryc](https://www.benfryc.com/), and developed by [Work Louder](https://worklouder.cc/). Evoking the look of both classic and modern technology, the k•no•b•1 is designed to be a beautiful and functional part of your every day workflow.
