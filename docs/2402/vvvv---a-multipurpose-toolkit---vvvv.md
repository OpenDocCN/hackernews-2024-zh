# vvvv - a multipurpose toolkit | vvvv

> 来源：[https://vvvv.org/](https://vvvv.org/)

| vvvv is a hybrid visual/textual live-programming environment for easy prototyping and development. It is designed to facilitate the handling of large media environments with physical interfaces, real-time motion graphics, audio and video that can interact with many users simultaneously. Responsible for its development is the [vvvv group](https://vvvv.org/impressum). | vvvv is **free for non-commercial use,** **commercial use requires a [license](https://vvvv.org/documentation/licensing)**. |
