Al Jazeera加沙分社社长Al Dahdouh在报道以色列每晚对联合国学校（该校收容了中心加沙南部汗尤尼斯的流离失所者）的轰炸后，[被无人机袭击](https://cpj.org/2023/12/al-jazeera-cameraperson-samer-abu-daqqa-killed-correspondent-wael-al-dahdouh-injured-in-drone-attack-in-khan-yunis/#:~:text=Al%20Dahdouh%20was%20hit%20by,of%20his%20colleague%20Abu%20Daqqa.)。根据[Al-Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.net/news/2023/12/15/%d8%b9%d8%a7%d8%ac%d9%84-%d9%85%d8%b1%d8%a7%d8%b3%d9%84-%d8%a7%d9%84%d8%ac%d8%b2%d9%8a%d8%b1%d8%a9-%d8%a5%d8%b5%d8%a7%d8%a8%d8%a9-%d8%a7%d9%84%d8%b2%d9%85%d9%8a%d9%84-%d9%88%d8%a7%d8%a6%d9%84)，[中东之眼](https://www.middleeasteye.net/news/israel-palestine-war-israeli-drone-strike-wounds-al-jazeera-journalists-gaza)，和[路透社](https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/two-al-jazeera-journalists-wounded-gaza-missile-strike-reporter-2023-12-15/)的报道，Dahdouh的手和腰部被弹片击中，他在汗尤尼斯的纳赛尔医院接受治疗。他的同事、摄像操作员Samer Abu Daqqa在同一次袭击中丧生。

Mustafa Alkharouf
