# Google fires more workers who protested its deal with Israel - The Washington Post

> 来源：[https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2024/04/22/google-nimbus-israel-protest-fired-workers/](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2024/04/22/google-nimbus-israel-protest-fired-workers/)

SAN FRANCISCO — Google fired about 20 more workers whom it said participated in protests denouncing the company’s cloud computing deal with the Israeli government, bringing the total number of workers [fired in the past week](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2024/04/18/google-fired-nimbus-israel-palestine/?itid=lk_inline_manual_2) over the issue to more than 50, according to the activist group representing the workers.
