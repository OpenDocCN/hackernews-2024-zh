# 'So hot you can't breathe': Extreme heat hits the Philippines - The Japan Times

> 来源：[https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2024/04/24/asia-pacific/philippines-extreme-heat/](https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2024/04/24/asia-pacific/philippines-extreme-heat/)

马尼拉 -

2024年星期三，菲律宾遭受极端高温，迫使成千上万所学校停课，发出警告，要求民众尽量减少户外活动时间。

3月、4月和5月通常是这个群岛国家最炎热和最干燥的月份，但今年的情况由于厄尔尼诺气候现象而恶化。

"It's so hot you can't breathe," said Erlin Tumaron, 60, who works at a seaside resort in Cavite province, south of Manila, where the heat index reached 47 degrees Celsius on Tuesday.
