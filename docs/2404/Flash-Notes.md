# Welcome to Flash Notes

### Transform Your Learning Experience

#### Why Flash Notes?

In a world overflowing with generic learning tools, Flash Notes stands out by offering a highly personalised and efficient learning experience tailored to advanced learners. Available on iOS and MacOS, Flash Notes is not just an app; it's your gateway to mastering new concepts and vocabulary, whenever and wherever you choose.

#### Intelligent and Intuitive Learning

*   **AI-Powered Flashcards:** Quickly convert your notes into dynamic flashcards. Short on time? Our premium ChatGPT integration crafts compelling prompts for you, making learning engaging and effective.
*   **Tailored Learning Algorithms:** Inspired by the spaced repetition method, Flash Notes adapts to your learning pace, ensuring you learn efficiently, based on your schedule.
*   **Seamless Multi-Device Synchronisation:** With CRDT technology, your notes are consistently synced across iOS and MacOS, ensuring smooth transitions from your office Mac to your iPhone on the go.

#### Privacy-Centric Design

Your data is your own. Stored securely in iCloud, Flash Notes guarantees that your learning journey remains private and accessible across all your devices.

Forget one-size-fits-all. With Flash Notes, your learning is as unique as you are. Whether it’s new professional terminology or intriguing concepts, our app is designed to help you absorb and retain knowledge effectively.
