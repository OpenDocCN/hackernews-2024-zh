# The Princeton Companion to Applied Mathematics – Nick Higham

> 来源：[https://nhigham.com/the-princeton-companion-to-applied-mathematics/](https://nhigham.com/the-princeton-companion-to-applied-mathematics/)

The article VIII.3 “How to Write a General Interest Mathematics” by Ian Stewart and the four articles grouped under VIII.7 Teaching Applied Mathematics (Gilbert Strang: “What to Teach and How”?, Peter R. Turner: “Computation, Modeling, and Projects”, David Acheson: “What’s the Big Picture?”, and IV. Rachel Levy: “Industrial Mathematics Inspires Mathematical Modeling Tasks”) were selected for reprinting in [The Best Writing on Mathematics 2016](https://press.princeton.edu/titles/10953.html).
