# VAIO Library

> 来源：[https://vaiolibrary.com/index.php/Welcome_to_the_VAIO_Library](https://vaiolibrary.com/index.php/Welcome_to_the_VAIO_Library)

##### **The VAIO Library is the best place to be for any VAIO related things!**

Our goal is to make a wiki with all the Sony VAIO models documented, with drivers and recovery media discs available to download for free. To learn more, you can visit our [about page](/index.php/VAIO_Library:About "VAIO Library:About"). We hope you enjoy your visit on the site!

**Please note that this site is still in development. Please check [development status](/index.php/Development_Status "Development Status").**

Recovery media discs are available on the [Recovery Discs](/index.php/Recovery_Discs "Recovery Discs") page.

For supporting the VAIO Library, please take a look at our [donations page](/index.php/Donate "Donate")! Even small donations are appreciated.

**Please see the menu to discover some useful pages,** like driver downloading guides, useful links and resources, or disassembly guides. **This is located on the top left corner.**

## Sony VAIO Series List

For series with devices that were released in different years, the series will be listed in the year of the series first appearance.
