# Founding Software Engineer at OfOne | Y Combinator

> 来源：[https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/ofone/jobs/u2E2fCX-founding-software-engineer](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/ofone/jobs/u2E2fCX-founding-software-engineer)

**About OfOne:**

At OfOne, we're reshaping the drive-thru experience with voice AI. We’re already helping top-50 restaurant chains operate more efficiently and level-up their customer experience.

We're on the lookout for a founding software engineer ready to *drive* into the voice AI scene. We’re offering a chance to deliver software that touches millions of people, pushes $billions of economic impact, and is at the forefront of delivering real-time AI applications to the real world.

We move fast, believe in collaboration over competition, and have the backing of major VCs like SignalFire, Correlation Ventures, and Y Combinator.

If this sounds like a fit, come join us at our office in the heart of SF and let's build something great.

**Responsibilities:**

*   Design, develop, and maintain scalable, high-performance backend systems for our automated order-taking platform
*   Collaborate closely with our team to ensure the seamless integration of our backend with our ML models and client devices
*   Monitor and optimize the performance of our backend systems in production environments
*   Build and maintain integrations with 3rd party restaurant software systems like POS, loyalty, payment gateways, and customer data platforms.
*   Implement best practices in system design, code quality, and testing to ensure a reliable, secure, and maintainable system

**Requirements:**

*   Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   4+ years of hands-on experience in developing, implementing, and maintaining backend infrastructure in production environments
*   Proven track record of building and deploying scalable, high-performance backend systems
*   Experience with cloud-based infrastructure and deployment technologies, such as AWS and API design.
*   Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to adapt to a rapidly evolving startup environment
*   Strong communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively with a team
*   Willingness to problem solve across our entire product
*   Ability to proactively recognize problems and identify solutions to those problems

**Nice-to-haves:**

*   Experience working with audio
*   Experience taking a backend system from zero to one
*   Experience building and maintaining a system that integrates with many 3rd party APIs, particularly Point of Sale systems
*   Experience with Kotlin or Java
*   Experience with containerization tools, such as Docker and Kubernetes

**What we offer:**

*   Competitive salary and equity package
*   Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance
*   Opportunities for growth and advancement within a fast-paced, innovative startup environment building on the cutting edge
*   The chance to make a meaningful impact on an industry ripe for innovation
*   Free lunch
