# My son and 27 others have died at skydiving centre. How is it still open?

> 来源：[https://www.thetimes.co.uk/article/skydiving-death-center-california-cm26bkd9k](https://www.thetimes.co.uk/article/skydiving-death-center-california-cm26bkd9k)

Before climbing into a plane for a skydive in California, Tyler Turner hugged his mother and told her: “I love you.”

They would be the last words Francine Turner heard from her 18-year-old son, who died shortly afterwards in August 2016, when the parachute he shared with an instructor failed to open.

Tyler’s is one of 28 deaths to have occurred at the Lodi Parachute Center since 1985, though Turner said she had no idea of the business’s fatality record when her son’s plane took off.

The 60-year-old, who lives in California’s San Joaquin Valley with her husband, Todd, is campaigning for the tighter regulation of skydiving and cannot understand why the centre remains open.
