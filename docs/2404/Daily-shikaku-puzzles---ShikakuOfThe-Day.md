# Daily shikaku puzzles | ShikakuOfThe.Day

> 来源：[https://shikakuofthe.day/https://jalopnik.com/a-tiktoker-got-trapped-inside-her-115-degree-tesla-mode-1851404777](https://shikakuofthe.day/https://jalopnik.com/a-tiktoker-got-trapped-inside-her-115-degree-tesla-mode-1851404777)

**HOW TO PLAY:** You have to divide the grid into rectangular and square pieces such that each piece contains exactly one number, and that number represents the area of the rectangle.

**Desktop:** Click and drag to draw a rectangle. Right click to remove a rectangle.
**Mobile:** Select a starting point then press where you want to draw a rectangle. Press and hold to remove a rectangle.
